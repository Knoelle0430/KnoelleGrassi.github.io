November 18, 2024

The Dirichlet Problems

This project applies complex analysis to solve classical Dirichlet problems in the upper half-plane and in the first quadrant. Using boundary conditions with temperature discontinuities, I constructed harmonic functions via the argument function, logarithmic potentials, and conformal maps. By expressing the solutions as imaginary parts of analytic functions, I verified harmonicity and generated visualizations of isotherms and heat-flux lines using MATLAB.

Tools & Skills Used

  • Mathematics: complex analysis, harmonic functions, Dirichlet problems, analytic continuation
  • Techniques: conformal mapping, boundary value problem formulation, argument functions, logarithmic potentials
  • Tools: MATLAB for contour plots, symbolic computation
  • Analysis: constructing analytic functions Ω(z), verifying harmonicity, interpreting level curves (isotherms & heat flux)
  • Visualization: contour plots of real/imaginary parts, mapping boundary discontinuities
